Explore The CEED

Chitkara University, Himachal Pradesh’s Centre for Entrepreneurship Education & Development (CEED), is poised to make a mark as it embarks on a transformative journey at ‘Startup Mahakumbh,’ a pioneering three-day event slated to unfold at Bharat Mandapam, Pragati Maidan, New Delhi. Supported by industry stakeholders and organisations including the National Association of Software and Service Companies (NASSCOM), Indian Venture and Alternate Capital Association (IVCA), TiE Global, Bootstrap Foundation, Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Invest India, Startup India, and the National Startup Advisory Council under the Ministry of Education, ‘Startup Mahakumbh’ promises to be a gathering like no other.
