Mehandi

The Mehndi tree, also known as Henna, is a vital plant in Ayurvedic medicine...

  • Dye hair and skin
  • Treat skin conditions like eczema and acne
  • Provide cooling and anti-inflammatory relief

Curry Patta

The Curry Patta tree, also known as Curry Leaf Tree, is a sacred herb in Indian cuisine...

  • Add flavor and aroma to curries and dishes
  • Aid digestion and relieve stomach issues
  • Lower cholesterol and blood sugar levels
Know More

Kapoor

The Kapoor tree, also known as Camphor Tree, is a highly valued plant in traditional medicine...

  • Treat various ailments, including fever, cough, and skin conditions
  • Possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties
  • Used as a natural insect repellent and pesticide
Know More

Dalchini

"The Dalchini tree, also known as Cinnamon, is a highly valued spice tree in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its bark is used to:

  • Treat digestive issues, diabetes, and respiratory problems
  • Possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties
  • Support weight loss, improve blood circulation, and boost immunity
